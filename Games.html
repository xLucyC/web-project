Galactic Odyssey: A Text Based Space Survival RPG.

This Game is a python 3 based game with backend only, it was created as part of a group project in which I was the lead programmer.
The goal of this game is to find your way back home without running out of Fuel and maybe even finding hidden secrets along the way.
Feel free to play the game or browse the code to see how it was put together.



Uniq

Uniq is a game i started development on a while ago with my brother in law, it is a 2.5D platformer where the paths and ability to overcome obstacles is decided by your choices in character creation.
This game offers a unique way to play and explore the strenghts and weaknesses of everyone whilst showing everyone can reach their goal by taking advantage of their strengths.

Unfortunately due to technical issues and a hard drive issue and devlopment of hte game was lost other than a few screenshots and progress videos, however due to this the game was
originally being built on Unity and now can switch and start development in Unreal 5.2 which should improve the outcome of the game. This also gave a chance to reflect on past experience
and look at ways in which the game can be built up foundationally to improve it overall.
